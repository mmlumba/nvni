Program Content

CENAs are taught basic nursing skills to assist the licensed nurse. These include communication skills, infection control, resident's rights, first aid, common illnesses, safety and emergency procedures, behavior disorders, resident daily needs, care of environment, nutrition/fluids, elimination, charting, and many more.

Goals

Our goal is to help our students become successful and competent in the basic skills of a Nursing Assistant. We center our program on building up each individual's confidence level and we offer a learning environment that is conducive to growth.

Upon successful completion of the program students will receive a Certificate of Completion. The certificate verifies that the student has met the requirements set forth by the state Commonwealth of Virgina and is eligible to take the State test to become a State Certified Evaluated Nursing Assistant (CENA).
